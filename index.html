Timetable Studio

A clean monochrome timetable builder for courses, labs, tutorials, practicals, work blocks, and weekly planning.

How course toggles work:
A course can contain lecture, tutorial, and practical blocks. Turning the course off disables all of those blocks together. Disabled courses can still appear faintly as shadows, but they do not count toward conflicts, busy hours, or free days.
